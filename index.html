About Levent Güneş

Levent Güneş is a musician with over 30 years of experience in ethnic music and sound design. His work includes composing scores for TV series and films, alongside various live performances.

About Karaca Music

Founded by Levent Güneş, Karaca Music has been at the forefront of preserving and sharing ethnic music traditions. The company specializes in creating soundtracks for film and television, as well as producing unique musical projects.
