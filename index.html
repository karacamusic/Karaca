About Karaca Music

Karaca Music was founded in 2019 by Levent Güneş in London and has quickly become a leading archive and production center for ethnic music.

🎶 Ethnic Music Archive: Karaca Music is dedicated to preserving the rich musical heritage of Anatolia and its surrounding regions.

🎬 Film & TV Soundtracks: The company has composed and produced music for national and international film and television projects.

🎤 Live Performances: Karaca Music brings traditional melodies to modern audiences through live performance recordings.

Karaca Music continues to serve as a bridge between cultures, blending the universal language of music with deep-rooted traditions.
